Buying This Beauty

I was lost in the stress of work never taking a break from my job and one day I looked at my cousin and said I wanna buy a boat. I looked on cragislist that day and found this beauty. I believe it was supposed to happen. When we arrived the guy said it was a good boat he got for his girls to play with in the canal; but they wanted a boat they could get pulled behind in a tube. I purchased the boat and could not stop smiling all the way home. My brain was spinning thinking about how do I want to build it out.

Customizing the Boat

I started off by removing the bow of the boat and the two benches with a angle grinder. The bout still had a stearing wheel although it was not connected. The boat came with a 14hp mercury out board motor. Then I got 2x4's and reinforced the floor of the boat and build the frame for the deck. Once the frame was done I layed plywood down and built the bench for my seats and cut a hold for the gas tank. Then I layed marrien cappet down and installed the seats. The third seat(the one up at the front) came off one day while i was leaning to far back. I reinforced the seat since. There was a bimini top I installed but it broke off when I didnt properly strap it down on the way home one day. I added a metal box to keep organized but its heavy so I will be thinking of a replacement. Most of my items are under the deck that has more than enough room.

The Results

I have a way to get away from town where I can think and be closer to my spiritual side. I have had friends and family come out. Building this boat is one of the best things I have done in while. Although I call it a fishing boat I use it more for a ecape from the city. (click the rock on the home page)
